About Me

Meet the baker behind La Dulce Monarca.

Hi, I'm Valerie V, the baker and creative heart behind La Dulce Monarca. I create custom cakes and handcrafted desserts designed to make every celebration extra special. From elegant mini wedding cakes and personalized cakes to fun treats, dessert tables, and sweet carts, I love bringing beautiful and delicious ideas to life.

What started as a creative outlet has grown into something I truly enjoy, and each dessert is made with care, attention to detail, and a personal touch. Whether you’re celebrating a milestone or simply treating yourself, I’m here to make your sweetest moments unforgettable.
